Thank you for your interest in Prep'd 2 Go!! So you have found our website and now need to know how it works--well your in luck. Prep'd 2 Go offers weekly meal prep service of fully prepared meals that are easy to reheat and enjoy. Each meal includes one entree and 2 sides.

Each Sunday we publish 3 meal options available for purchase. These can be found under our Menu page. Orders are place between Sunday and Thursday. All orders and payments must be submitted by Thursday at 6pm. The minimal number of orders allowed is 5. You may order an combination of meals from the weekly menu. We also carry extra sides that can be added to your order.

Orders can be place by phone or using are online order form. Orders are available for pick-up on Sundays between 5:30-8:30pm. Don't feel like coming out, no problem. You can request delivery of your meals for a $5 delivery fee.Orders being delivered to employment locations with more than 3 orders are free of charge.
